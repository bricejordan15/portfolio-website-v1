PORTFOLIO ABOUT CONTACT

My name's Brice and I design solutions.

Over the years I've gotten pretty good at using design to solve problems.

I've worked in the industry since 2017, both as a freelancer and in-house designer. At my previous position at 365 Holdings, I designed for six e-commerce brands (simultaneously!) where I worked on branding, packaging, websites, emails, advertisements, infographics, animations, you name it. I was part of a fast-paced marketing team of 15+ as the sole graphic designer, and each day required me to step up to the plate to take on the next challenge.

I pride myself as an efficient and communicative professional with a team-centric mindset. I believe that designers in today's industry need to be swiss-army knives; constantly learning new skills to fill in the gaps whenever necessary. I'm always adding new skills to my creative arsenal, it's one of the most fun parts about being a designer!

In my free time, you can find me hiking, working out, gaming with friends, or spending time with family and my two insane cats.

I'm currently seeking new design work. Head over to the Contact page if you'd like to reach out! Thanks for stopping by!
